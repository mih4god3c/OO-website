Early renaissance
Early renaissance

In Early Renaissance, our algorithm managed to cluster the artworks into three main clusters. One cluster is made out of painitngs of Madonna holding a child, second cluster is represented by portraits and the third cluster consits of painitngs of Crucification. Portraits are painted from the shoulders up, with subjects not facing straight to the observer. We can see that the subjects are depicted with more anatomical realism versus the earlier art. Most of the motives are connected to religion, which is also very standard for the period of Early renaissance.

Some famous artists that were active in this period are: Masaccio, Donatello, Fra Angelico, Sandro Botticelli, Giovani Bellini.

Some famous art works from this period are: Expulsion from the garden of Eden, The Holy Trinity, The Annuciation, Flagelation of Christ, Primavera.

High renaissance
High renaissance

Classification of High renaissance paintings, looks very similar to both other renaissance periods. We can see that there are again the same three clusters as before. There is a slight difference in portraits, where we noticed that more subjects are faicing straight to the observer. High renaissance is a period dominated with religious motives. There are still some differences between the High and the Early renaissance. Human figures are painted much more divine, and there is also more use of the linear perspective and shading.

Some famous artists that were active in this period are: Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, Raphael, Andrea del Sarto, Correggio.

Some famous art works from this period are: Virgin of the rocks, The Last Supper, Mona Lisa, The Alba Madonna, School of Athens, The creation of Adam, The Sistine Madonna.

Baroque
Baroque

For the period of Baroque, our algorithm managed to cluster the painitngs into the next three clusters. Poses where the subject is in laying position, portraits and painitngs containing standing subjects. We can see less religious motives and more emphasis on the human body. Paintings are also much more emphasizing beauty and majesty. In laying poses there are a lot of paintings representing a naked woman. We can also notice that portraits are drawn from further away. As for the standing poses, we can see that our algorithm still classified the painitng of Crusification as the best fit to the calculated median pose.

Some famous artists that were active in this period are: Caravaggio, Peter Paul Rubens, Artemisia Gentileschi, Gian Lorenzo Bernini, Rembrandt van Rijn.
